The Founder

Robert Timmer is an independent boardroom advisor specialising in strategy, organisational development, and human–AI collaborative decision-making.

With over two decades of experience building and leading organisations across the Netherlands, Europe, Africa, and Asia-Pacific, Robert brings a rare combination of strategic depth and operational reality to boardroom conversations.

Through The Paradox, he works with boards and leadership teams navigating complex strategic choices, organisational transitions, and the governance challenges that come with increasing AI involvement in decision processes.

He is completing an EMBA at Nyenrode Business University, with his thesis research focused on human–AI collaborative decision-making in governance contexts.

What we stand for

Operating constraints, not aspirations.

These are not positioning statements. They are the conditions under which the work is done.

01

Intellectual honesty over comfort

We distinguish between what the evidence shows and what we believe. Where the data supports a claim, we make it clearly. Where it does not, we say so.

02

Substance before visibility

We publish when we have something to say, not when we need to be seen. Every keynote, paper, and engagement is grounded in research that earns its claim.

03

Independence as a design principle

We are not on call. We are thinkers that boards access on structured, scoped terms. Independence protects the quality of the work and the integrity of the advice.

Vision

Decision quality as a designed capability.

A world where every consequential decision — in boardrooms, institutions, systems that shape lives — is made with awareness of both human and artificial limitations.
