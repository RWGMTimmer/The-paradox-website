The Paradox

AI was built to improve decisions.

In practice, ungoverned AI introduces invisible distortions that make decision-makers more confident without making them more correct. The tool designed to sharpen judgment can quietly blunt it.

Every board is now a human–AI decision system. Most do not know it yet. The competitive advantage in AI is not capability. It is knowing where human and AI judgment both break down, and designing for it.

WHERE WE WORK

Where human judgment meets artificial intelligence.

The Paradox works on what happens to decision quality when AI enters the room, and what to do about it. Not compliance checklists. Not algorithm audits. The structural dynamics of the decision itself.
