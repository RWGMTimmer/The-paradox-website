The Paradox is not built on opinion. The framework emerges from original qualitative research with executives and board members across sectors, interrogating what actually happens to decision quality when AI enters the room.

Featured Inquiry

Dual Bounded Rationality

Humans and AI each operate under distinct cognitive constraints. Their interaction in decision processes does not cancel these limitations. It compounds them. The research traces cascading dynamics where cognitive offloading to AI progressively degrades the judgment it was meant to support, and identifies the design principles that counter this erosion.

A practitioner-facing working paper is in preparation for publication in 2026.
